About Me

Hi! I'm Emanuel, a self-taught web developer passionate about building real, functional solutions through code. I began my programming journey in November 2024 and quickly discovered my love for backend development.

Since then, I’ve been focusing on Python and Flask to create practical applications — like automation tools and SaaS platforms. My goal is to become a backend specialist, but I'm also learning frontend basics so I can bring my ideas to life from start to finish.

I enjoy solving problems, organizing systems, and constantly challenging myself to learn more. I believe that every line of code I write brings me closer to becoming a professional developer.

Outside of programming, I’m always thinking about new ideas, improving existing projects, and preparing for my next step into the tech industry.
